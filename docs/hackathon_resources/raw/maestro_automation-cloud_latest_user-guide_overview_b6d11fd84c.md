 Overview

 link What is Maestro? ​

 link
 UiPath Maestro™ is a cloud-native orchestration platform that unifies automation, AI agents, and human interactions into streamlined, end-to-end business processes.

 It allows organizations to model and orchestrate work using two complementary approaches: BPMN (Business Process Model and Notation) for structured, sequential workflows, and Case Management for long-running, exception-heavy work that cannot be fully defined upfront. Both approaches share the same UiPath platform services and can work together—a case can invoke a BPMN process as one of its task types. To choose between the two approaches, see When to use BPMN Process vs. Case Management .

 Maestro also supports defining business rules with DMN (Decision Model and Notation), and coordinates multiple actors—including RPA bots, AI tools, and people—within a single process.

 This approach replaces disconnected automation tasks with intelligent, governed workflows that are easier to manage, adapt, and scale.

 Enterprise capabilities ​

 link
 Designed for enterprise use, Maestro offers real-time monitoring, centralized control, and built-in compliance tools, making it ideal for complex, long-running workflows. It supports live instance supervision (pause, resume, retry), detailed analytics, and continuous optimization through process mining. By providing a single platform to orchestrate agentic workflows, Maestro helps organizations improve operational efficiency, reduce manual work, and accelerate digital transformation.

 Maestro serves as a mechanism to enhance the performance of your automated workforce and systems. It combines process data with task and automation-level data to better understand your processes.
