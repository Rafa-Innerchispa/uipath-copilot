 What are the differences between online and in-person hackathons? While in-person events are great for local networking and students, online competitions are superior for driving business value. Online events reach a global audience of experienced developers, run for longer durations, and consistently deliver more polished, production-ready applications.

 What is the typical conversion rate from registration to project submission? While traditional marketing channels see conversion rates below 1%, Devpost hackathons consistently deliver a 5–33% conversion rate.
