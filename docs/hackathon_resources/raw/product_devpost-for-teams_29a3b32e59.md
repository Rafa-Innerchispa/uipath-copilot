 Contact Us What is Devpost for Teams? Devpost for Teams is a secure, enterprise-grade platform designed for running internal and customer-facing hackathons. It provides a centralized hub to manage registration, project submissions, judging, and voting in a private environment.

 What is Devpost for Teams’ pricing model? We offer an annual subscription based on the number of seats you require. This model allows you to run unlimited hackathons throughout the year. We also offer optional managed services and professional support.

 I’ve never run a hackathon before. Can you help me plan or manage ours? Yes. Every subscription includes platform onboarding, a best-practices toolkit, and platform support. For organizations that need deeper assistance, our Pro Services provide additional consultation, judging support, and a dedicated Customer Success Manager.

 Is there a limit to the number of users we can have on Devpost for Teams? No. You can add as many users as your organization requires. Your subscription is based on the number of seats purchased; if your needs grow, you can easily scale your plan to accommodate more team members.

 Are voters counted toward the seat total on Devpost for Teams? Voters are not counted toward your seat total on our platform.

 Who can create a hackathon? Any internal Admin , Organizer , or Member can create a hackathon. Once a user creates an event, they are automatically granted &quot;Organizer&quot; permissions for that specific hackathon.
‍
- Admins &amp; Members: Can initiate new hackathons at any time.
- Guests: External participants (customers/partners) cannot create hackathons. However, you can assign them a &quot;Guest Organizer&quot; role to help manage a specific event they have been invited to. Can I invite and control access for external guests? Yes. You have full control over who joins your event. You can securely invite customers or partners by pre-approving guest lists or by requiring participants to request approval before they are granted access to your private hackathon.
